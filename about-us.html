About us

Founded by a Marine Corps veteran nearly twenty years ago, Mr Clean Maintenance Company LLC has become an active member of the Building Service Contractors’ Association International and easily distinguishable from the surrounding commercial cleaning firms by our unwavering dedication to excellence and advancement; we stand by the notion that it’s only a job well done if it exceeds clients’ expectations. A certified minority HUB of North Carolina , Mr Clean Maintenance Company LLC follows a business model rooted in the belief that a local firm has the responsibility to engage with the entire community while serving as an example of upstanding character by the dedication to the maintenance of a nondiscriminatory workforce.
